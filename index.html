About Us

Cherwel Caravan Services are a family owned business, based on the border of Cambridgeshire and Norfolk, who have been in the caravan and motorhome industry for over 11 years.

Paul started out at age 16 in the automotive industry, fitting components to HGV's and spent years fixing his own vehicles and caravans along the way. After 33 years, and countless caravan holidays with his family, Paul decided to follow his passion and became a qualified, MCEA approved caravan engineer.

From its launch in 2012, Cherwel Caravan Services has continuously grown and has gained a loyal customer base. Our aim is to provide a quality service whilst ensuring your caravan or motorhome is safe and roadworthy, so you can continue to enjoy it for years to come.
