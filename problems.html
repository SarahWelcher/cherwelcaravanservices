At Cherwel Caravan Services, we know how worrying it can be when something isn't working as it should be in your caravan or motorhome.

Thats why, we are happy to try and help you over the phone if you prefer before booking in for us to come out and have a look at the problem.

Below is some information that might help if you come across any of these issues.

Fridge Failure

  1. If the fridge locks out, the first thing to check is to make sure you haven't run out of gas, and check your gas bottle.
  2. If it still persists, turn the fridge off, reset the fridge, try again.
  3. Try this up to 4 times to allow gas to get through.
  4. If it doesn't, further investigation is needed.

Water Ingress

If you haven't used your caravan for a while and begin to smell a musty smell, you could have water ingress.
This basically means there is a good chance water is getting in between the panels of your caravan.
It's best to locate water ingress sooner rather than later to prevent expensive repair bills, and you can check this yourself with a damp meter.
